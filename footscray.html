The Footscray shortlist.

For a morning walk

Footscray Park, along the Maribyrnong

Especially on a quiet Tuesday.

For coffee

Rudimentary, Nicholson Street

Not before one o’clock on weekends. Worth the wait.

For a cold Saturday

West Footscray Library, then Migrant Coffee

Storytime, a deep children's shelf, and a bagel four doors along Barkly Street.

Footscray train (direct from City Loop, 8 min)
