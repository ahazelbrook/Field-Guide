About

A small, honest list, made on Saturdays.

We started Field Guide because we kept being recommended things that were either for the children or for us, and almost never for both of us together.

Field Guide is a weekend newsletter for parents in their mid-thirties living in Melbourne's inner west. One Saturday morning, one carefully chosen weekend — a botanic garden made for slow mornings, a workshop the kids will remember, a coffee good enough to justify the queue.

We don't list fifty things. We list the few that actually worked. Curated, never exhaustive — the small, honest list of things worth keeping.
